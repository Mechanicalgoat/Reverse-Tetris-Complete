How to Play

Objective

Send pieces to the AI and try to fill the top 3 rows to win. But be careful - every piece you send costs points!

Scoring System

  • Start with initial points based on difficulty
  • Lose points for every piece you send
  • Lose more points when AI clears lines
  • Your score can go negative!
  • Challenge: Keep your score as high as possible

Controls

  • Click piece buttons or use keys 1-7
  • Send pieces rapidly for speed bonus
  • Press Space to pause
